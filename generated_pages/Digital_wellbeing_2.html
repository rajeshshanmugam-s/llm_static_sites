Digital Wellbeing

What is Digital Wellbeing?

Digital wellbeing refers to maintaining a healthy and balanced relationship with digital technologies. It involves managing screen time, reducing digital distractions, and using technology in ways that enhance rather than hinder your mental, emotional, and physical health.

Why is it Important?

In our hyper-connected world, excessive screen time and constant notifications can lead to stress, anxiety, and reduced productivity. Prioritizing digital wellbeing helps you regain control over your time, improve focus, and foster real-world connections.

How to Improve Your Digital Wellbeing

  • Set boundaries for screen time using device settings or apps.
  • Designate tech-free zones or times (e.g., during meals or before bed).
  • Practice mindfulness by taking regular digital detox breaks.
  • Use apps to track usage and identify unhealthy patterns.
  • Engage in offline hobbies and activities to rebalance your routine.
